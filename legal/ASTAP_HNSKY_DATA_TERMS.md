# ASTAP/HNSKY catalogue distribution terms

The ASTAP/HNSKY catalogue formats and catalogue packages are developed
and maintained by Han Kleijn.

Written permission was received from Han Kleijn on 20 July 2026 to:

- use ASTAP/HNSKY databases as intermediate data for generating
  ZeSolver indexes;
- redistribute ASTAP/HNSKY databases;
- redistribute ZeSolver-derived catalogue tiles, quad tables and indexes;

subject to the following conditions:

1. The distributed catalogue data and derived assets must not be used
   for commercial purposes.
2. Credit must be given to Han Kleijn, ASTAP and HNSKY.
3. The applicable ESA/Gaia/DPAC copyright and attribution text must
   accompany every redistribution.

These conditions apply to catalogue data and catalogue-derived assets.
They do not modify the GNU GPL licence governing the ZeSolver source code.
